Frequently Asked Questions

What does Valumedia Do

We are Nigeria's top transit advertising firm. Our services include exterior and interior branding, as well as BRT-TV and RAIL-TV on buses, trains, and cabs.

How do you handle maintenance and upkeep of ad displays on transit vehicles?

Regular communication with bus operators helps us to ensure that any maintenance needs are addressed promptly. Additionally we carry out inspections for any damages or malfunctions, cleaning of the displays to ensure clear visibility, and prompt repairs as needed.

Are there any restrictions or regulations on the content of transit/Tv ads?

Yes, there are typically restrictions and regulations on the content of transit and TV ads. Inappropriate content: Ads must not contain obscene, offensive, or inappropriate material. Misleading information: Ads must not make false or misleading claims.
